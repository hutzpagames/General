Privacy Policy for Hutzpa Games

This Privacy Policy describes how **Hutzpa Games** ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our website, games (including **'Tip of the Tongue'**), and services (collectively, the "Services"). By using the Services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two main types of information:

  • **Information You Provide (Directly):** This includes information submitted via contact forms (e.g., name, email address, project details) and **User-Generated Content (UGC)** such as custom words or suggestions submitted through the games.
  • **Information Collected Automatically (Usage Data):** When you access the Services, we automatically collect usage data, including your IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, pages viewed, time spent, and referral sources (via Google Analytics and other tracking technologies).

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use Cookies, Google Analytics, and similar technologies to collect information about your activities over time and across different websites. This is primarily done for:**

  • Analyzing traffic and usage patterns.
  • **Serving Advertisements (AdSense Compliance):** We partner with **Google AdSense** and other third-party ad networks. These partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.
  • We use **Cookiebot** for managing cookie consent.

You can manage your cookie preferences through the provided Cookiebot pop-up or by adjusting your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • **Operation:** To provide, maintain, and improve our games and services.
  • **Communication:** To respond to your inquiries and send marketing communications if you opted in.
  • **Monetization:** To serve targeted and non-targeted advertisements through Google AdSense. **Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.**
  • **UGC:** To incorporate user-submitted words/suggestions into our game databases to enhance content quality.

4. Sharing Your Information

We share your information with:

  • **Service Providers:** Google (Analytics, AdSense), and form providers (Tally), which process data on our behalf.
  • **Ad Partners:** Third-party advertisers and ad networks for the purpose of serving ads.
  • **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights or property.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from a child under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

6. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the contact form on our main page or by email at: info@hutzpa.games
